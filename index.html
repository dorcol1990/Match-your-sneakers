"Match Your Sneakers" is a memory test123123 card game that challenges players to match pairs of sneaker-themed cards from a deck of 36 cards (18 pairs). The game features six levels, each with progressively less time to complete the matching task. Players flip over two cards at a time, attempting to remember their location and match them to their respective pairs. The game's cards feature unique designs of sneakers.
